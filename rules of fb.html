Adjust how ads on Facebook are shown to you based on your activity
information from ad partners


1. Click your profile picture in the top right of Facebook.

2. Select Settings and privacy, then click Settings.

3. Click Accounts Centre, then click Ad preferences.

4. Click the Manage info tab.

5. Click Activity information from ad partners.

Click Review setting.

6. Choose whether we can show you ads based on your activity
information from ad partners.
